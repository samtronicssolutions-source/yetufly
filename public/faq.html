Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?
Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide your shipping information and choose a payment method (M-Pesa or Cash on Delivery).
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept M-Pesa (Lipa Na M-Pesa - STK Push) and Cash on Delivery. For M-Pesa payments, you'll receive a payment request on your phone to complete the transaction.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery times vary by location: Nairobi (1-2 business days), major towns (2-3 days), other counties (3-5 days). Orders over KSh 5,000 qualify for free shipping.
Can I return a product?
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Contact our support team to initiate a return.
How do I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via SMS and email. You can also check your order status by contacting our customer service.
What if my product is defective?
If you receive a defective product, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund at no extra cost.
Do you offer warranty on products?
Yes, most of our products come with a manufacturer's warranty. Warranty periods vary by product. Check the product description for details.
